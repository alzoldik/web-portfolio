About Candelaria

Crafting moments of serenity, one candle at a time.

At Candelaria, we believe in the power of ambiance. Our journey began with a simple passion: creating perfect moments through the art of candlemaking. Each candle is thoughtfully crafted using premium soy wax, pure essential oils, and cotton wicks, ensuring a clean, long-lasting burn.

Our commitment to quality and sustainability means we source only the finest natural ingredients while minimizing our environmental impact. Every Candelaria candle is hand-poured in small batches, allowing us to maintain exceptional quality and attention to detail.
